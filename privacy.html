Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Crafty Mouse Gifts ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your data.

Who We Are

Crafty Mouse Gifts is a handmade gifts business based in the Scottish Borders, Scotland. You can contact us at craftymousegifts@gmail.com.

What Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your delivery address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we never store card details)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Any messages you send us

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Improve our products and services
  • Send marketing emails (only with your consent)

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted third parties solely to fulfil your order, including:

  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Royal Mail / courier services (delivery)

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at craftymousegifts@gmail.com.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to function correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies without your consent.

Data Retention

We retain your order data for up to 7 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time subject to legal requirements.

Contact

If you have any concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us at craftymousegifts@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
