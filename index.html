Our Story

Hi, I'm Michaela

The maker behind Crafty Mouse Gifts, based in the beautiful Scottish Borders. What started as a passion for crafting has grown into a small business built around wellness, self-care and meaningful gifting.

Every candle, wax melt and eco-resin piece I create is handmade with love, using eco-friendly soy wax and plant-based materials. As a Reiki practitioner, I bring an extra layer of intention and positive energy to everything I make — because I believe the gifts we give should feel as good as they look.

Whether you're treating yourself or finding something special for someone you love, every CMG order is made by hand, just for you.
