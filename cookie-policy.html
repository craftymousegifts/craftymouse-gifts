Cookie Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use on craftymousegifts.com, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use them.

Cookies We Use

Cookie / StoragePurposeTypeDuration
cmg_cartStores your shopping basket so items are remembered as you browseEssentialUntil cleared

What We Don't Use

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Third-party social media cookies
  • Profiling or behavioural targeting cookies
  • Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies (like your shopping basket) are necessary for the website to function. They cannot be switched off as the site would not work without them. They do not store any personally identifiable information.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect any personal data. It simply counts visits and page views to help us improve our website. No consent is required for Plausible.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies (like the cart cookie) may affect how the site works for you. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please email contact@craftymousegifts.com.
